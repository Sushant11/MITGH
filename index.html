WHO WE ARE

About Us

MIT Group Holdings is an Australian private investment group with core strengths in education, heritage property restoration, and boutique hospitality. We are the parent company of Melbourne Institute of Technology and steward a portfolio of landmark properties across Australia, Europe, and Asia.

Founded in 2007, our group operates with a long-term perspective, focusing on investments that combine financial returns with meaningful social impact. Each of our businesses shares a commitment to excellence, innovation, and responsible stewardship.
